Mushu, more than just an ordinary scottish fold. He was the muse, the life, the joy to all. This coin was created for him a year ago, to help with his condition.

We worked for months... photoshoots, editing, boxes of kitty treats to help him behave. Mushu sadly passed away on October 2nd, 2025. So this coin now stands as a symbol, not of loss, but of legacy.

  • Сreate a wallet

    Download the MetaMask extension from the official website and install it in your browser. Fund it by transferring ETH from an exchange or another wallet.

  • Сonnect to dex

    Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap, SushiSwap, or 1inch. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet".Then select MetaMask

  • Swap eth for $MUSHU

    To swap ETH for Neiro, select ETH in the "from" field and choose Neiro in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

  • Verify & manage tokens

    Verify your transaction status in MetaMask under the "Activity" tab or on Etherscan using your Ethereum address. You import the token to your MetaMask by pasting in the CA.
